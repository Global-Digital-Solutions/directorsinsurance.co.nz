About DirectorsInsurance.co.nz

NZ's specialist D&O insurance comparison service — connecting directors with licensed insurance brokers to find the best cover at competitive prices.

Our Mission

directors carry significant personal liability under the Companies Act 1993, the Health and Safety at Work Act 2015, and other legislation — yet many serve on boards without adequate personal insurance protection.

DirectorsInsurance.co.nz was built to fix that. We provide clear, unbiased information about D&O and management liability insurance, and connect directors with specialist brokers who can arrange the right cover quickly and efficiently.

Our goal is simple: get directors speaking with the right broker, so they end up with cover that actually protects their personal assets — not just a cheap policy that fails when they need it most.

How We're Different

🎯

D&O Specialist Focus

We only deal with directors and officers insurance and management liability — not home, car, or pet insurance. That focus means we understand director risk better than a generalist.

🤝

Licensed Broker Network

Every broker in our network is licensed under the FMCA and specialises in D&O and management liability insurance. You get genuine expertise, not a call centre script.

🆓

Truly Free Service

There is no catch. We are paid a referral fee by brokers — you pay nothing extra and the competition between brokers keeps pricing competitive.

⚡

Fast Turnaround

We aim to connect you with a broker within 24 hours of your enquiry. Standard D&O cover can often be arranged the same day for straightforward requirements.

How Our Service Works

DirectorsInsurance.co.nz is a referral service. We do not sell insurance directly or act as a financial adviser. Instead, we connect you with licensed insurance brokers who are regulated under the Financial Markets Conduct Act 2013.

When you submit a quote request, we assess your director role, company type, and cover needs, then match you with the most appropriate specialist broker in our network. That broker contacts you to discuss your requirements in detail and provides comparative quotes from multiple D&O insurers.

We receive a referral fee from the broker if you proceed — you pay nothing additional. The broker is remunerated by commission from the insurer.
