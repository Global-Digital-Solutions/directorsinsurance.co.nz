Directors Insurance Wellington

Wellington directors face a unique risk environment — a high concentration of government-related entities, professional services firms, and not-for-profit organisations. Our Wellington network specialises in D&O and management liability for all these sectors.

Wellington's Unique Director Risks

Wellington directors frequently encounter Crown entity and state-owned enterprise governance requirements, charity and NFP trustee liability (Wellington has a particularly high density of charities), professional services exposure for legal, accounting, and consulting directors, and central government regulatory scrutiny.

Cover for Wellington NFPs

D&O insurance for Wellington charities, trusts, and incorporated societies is affordable and important. Trustees face the same personal liability as company directors. We can arrange cover from as little as $500/year for smaller organisations.
