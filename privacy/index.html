Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect

When you submit a quote request on this website, we collect your name, email address, phone number, company name, and details about the insurance cover you are seeking. This information is provided voluntarily by you.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to connect you with a licensed NZ insurance adviser who can help with your insurance needs. We do not use your information for marketing purposes without your consent and do not sell your information to third parties.

Who We Share Your Information With

Your details are shared with a licensed Financial Advice Provider (insurance adviser) in our referral network who will contact you to discuss your insurance requirements. Your information may also be shared with our email service provider (FormSubmit) for the purpose of delivering your enquiry.

Data Retention

We retain enquiry data for up to 24 months for the purpose of improving our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to access and correct personal information we hold about you. To make a request, contact us at hello@cover4you.co.nz.

Cookies

This website may use basic cookies for analytics purposes. No personally identifiable information is collected through cookies.

Contact

For privacy enquiries, contact us at hello@cover4you.co.nz .
